Our Process

We follow a streamlined approach to ensure efficient handling of your shipments from origin to destination.

1

Initial Consultation

We begin by understanding your specific requirements, cargo details, origins, destinations, and timelines to develop a tailored solution.

2

Documentation Preparation

Our team prepares and reviews all necessary customs documentation to ensure accuracy and compliance with international trade regulations.

3

Customs Classification

We determine the correct HS codes for your goods, calculate applicable duties and taxes, and prepare for customs submission.

4

Customs Clearance

We handle the submission of documents to customs authorities, address any inquiries, and ensure prompt clearance of your goods.

5

Transportation Management

Once cleared, we coordinate the transportation of your cargo to its final destination, providing real-time tracking throughout the journey.

6

Delivery & Follow-up

After successful delivery, we conduct a thorough review of the process and provide recommendations for future shipments.
